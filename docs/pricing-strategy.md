# **CarbonTrackAI — Pricing Strategy for LLM‑Powered ESG Reporting Platform**  
### *Internal Strategy Document — v1.0*

---

## **1. Overview**

CarbonTrackAI is an AI‑powered ESG reporting platform designed for European SMEs navigating CSRD and ESRS compliance. The platform combines:

- Automated data collection  
- Double materiality assessment  
- ESRS/EU Taxonomy mapping  
- LLM‑generated ESG narratives  
- Assurance‑ready reporting  

This document outlines the recommended pricing strategy that aligns with SME willingness‑to‑pay, regulatory pressure, and the value delivered by LLM‑powered automation.

---

## **2. Pricing Philosophy**

The pricing strategy is built on three principles:

### **2.1 Land with a free wedge**  
SMEs are cost‑sensitive and often unaware of their reporting obligations. A free tier removes friction and captures early‑stage users.

### **2.2 Expand through compliance needs**  
Once SMEs begin reporting, they naturally require additional ESRS modules, EU Taxonomy alignment, and assurance‑ready outputs.

### **2.3 Monetize LLM‑powered value**  
Materiality scoring, narrative generation, KPI extraction, and report drafting are high‑value features that justify premium tiers.

---

## **3. Target Customer Segments**

### **3.1 Micro‑SMEs (1–49 employees)**  
- Low budget  
- Need basic ESG readiness  
- Motivated by supply‑chain pressure  

### **3.2 Small & Medium Enterprises (50–249 employees)**  
- Preparing for CSRD  
- Need full ESRS coverage  
- Require structured reporting  

### **3.3 Consultants & Accountants**  
- Serve multiple SME clients  
- Need white‑label reporting  
- High willingness‑to‑pay

## **8. Pricing Risks & Mitigations**

### **Risk 1: SMEs resist subscription fees**  
Mitigation: Offer one‑off report pricing.

### **Risk 2: LLM costs scale unpredictably**  
Mitigation: Credit‑based usage model.

### **Risk 3: Competitors undercut pricing**  
Mitigation: Focus on CSRD/ESRS accuracy and assurance‑ready outputs.
